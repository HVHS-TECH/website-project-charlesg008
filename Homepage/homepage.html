October 25th & 26th

PLAY Arcade @ EVENT Queensgate

Kia ora! And welcome to the website of the first all-inclusive Pump it Up Tournament in Aotearoa New Zealand.
This multi-day, double-elimination style tournament is open to anyone and everyone.
Whether you've never played, are a seasoned vet, or have achieved the rank of「THE MASTER」,
come join us at PLAY Arcade, EVENT Cinema Queensgate on the 25th & 26th of October for 2 days of fun competition and community building!

In this double-elim style tournament, players will play 1 normal set worth of songs (3).
The scores from these 3 songs will be added up, and whomever's score is the highest wins!

We have 6 different categories for you to join based on skill level

Novice Singles, Levels 1 - 4
Novice Plus Singles, Levels 5 - 9
Intermediate Singles, Levels 10 - 16
Intermediate Plus Singles & Doubles, Levels 17 - 19
Advanced Singles & Doubles, Levels 20 - 21
Advanced Plus Singles & Doubles, Levels 22+
